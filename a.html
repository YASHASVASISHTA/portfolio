About Me

Get to know the creative mind behind these designs

Hello! I'm Elena, a passionate freelance designer with 5+ years of experience creating stunning visual identities for brands and businesses. My journey began at the School of Visual Arts in New York, where I discovered my love for blending aesthetics with functionality.


I specialize in UI/UX design, branding, and illustration, helping clients transform their ideas into visually compelling experiences. When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring art galleries or hiking with my camera.


My approach combines strategic thinking with artistic expression to deliver designs that not only look beautiful but also drive results.
